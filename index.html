Putting Jobs to Be Done into Practice

How to use
  1. Work through the three-step JTBD process below. Fill in all fields in detail.
  2. Use evidence (PESTLE, SWOT, persona, segment data) for every job. Anchor statements in real research or customer quotes.
  3. Score importance and satisfaction (1–10) for each job, then justify your prioritisation.
  4. Translate the highest opportunity job into a measurable outcome linked to your concept’s value proposition.
  5. When finished, Export to Word for a professional worksheet to submit.

1. JTBD Statement Crafting
